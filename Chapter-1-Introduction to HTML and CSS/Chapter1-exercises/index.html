Yellow Label Black Tea

This is a classic black tea recipe made with a tea bag, boiling water, and optional sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 Yellow Label tea bag
  • 500ml boiling water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (optional)

Method

  1. Place the tea bag in a teapot.
  2. Pour the boiling water over the tea bag.
  3. Let it steep for 3–5 minutes.
  4. Pour the tea into a cup.
  5. Add sugar if desired and stir well.
