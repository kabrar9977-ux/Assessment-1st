Yellow Label Black Tea

The Art of Perfect Tea Brewing

Classic Blend
⏱️ 3-5 min

About This Tea

Yellow Label Black Tea is a timeless classic that has been enjoyed by tea lovers worldwide for generations. This robust, full-bodied blend delivers a rich flavor profile with bright, brisk notes that make it the perfect morning or afternoon tea. Learn to brew it like a professional with our expert guide.

Ingredients & Equipment

Ingredients

  • 🫖 1 Yellow Label Black Tea bag 1 bag
  • 💧 Fresh boiling water 500ml
  • 🍯 Sugar (optional) 2 tbsp
  • 🥛 Milk (optional) 30ml

Equipment Needed

  • 🫖 Clean teapot or mug
  • ⏱️ Timer or watch
  • 🥄 Spoon for stirring

Professional Brewing Method

1

Prepare Your Tools

Start with a clean teapot or mug. Warm it by rinsing with hot water to maintain optimal brewing temperature.

💡 Pro tip: Pre-warming ensures your tea stays hot longer.
2

Place the Tea Bag

Gently place one Yellow Label Black Tea bag into your teapot or mug. Avoid squeezing the bag to prevent bitterness.

💡 Pro tip: Use fresh tea bags for best flavor.
3

Add Boiling Water

Pour 500ml of freshly boiled water (100°C / 212°F) directly over the tea bag. Ensure complete coverage.

💡 Pro tip: Use filtered water for better taste.
4

Steep to Perfection

Let the tea steep for exactly 3-5 minutes. Don't exceed 5 minutes to avoid bitterness.

⏰ Set a timer for perfect results every time.
5

Remove & Serve

Gently remove the tea bag without squeezing. Add sugar and milk to taste if desired.

💡 Pro tip: Squeezing releases bitter tannins.

Expert Tips & Variations

🌡️ Temperature Matters

Water should be at a rolling boil (100°C / 212°F) for optimal extraction of flavors.

⏰ Timing is Key

3-5 minutes is the sweet spot. Less time = weak tea, more time = bitter tea.

🥛 Milk First?

Add milk after removing the tea bag to prevent curdling and maintain flavor.

🍯 Sweet Alternatives

Try honey, agave, or stevia instead of sugar for different flavor profiles.

Serving Suggestions

Classic Black

Enjoy it pure and strong, the way tea purists prefer it.

With Milk

Add a splash of milk for a creamy, smooth experience.

Sweetened

Add sugar or honey to balance the robust flavor.

Iced Tea

Brew strong, then pour over ice for a refreshing summer drink.
